## 1. Key Trends & Definitions
*   **Agentic AI Definition**: Systems capable of autonomous goal achievement with minimal supervision, exhibiting adaptability and proactivity. Distinguished from traditional automation by its ability to handle dynamic, multistep tasks.
*   **Adoption**: Significant enterprise adoption in 2025 (79% of organizations reporting some level of use), with a focus on performance optimization and development assistance.
*   **"Hub-and-Spoke" Architecture**: A common design pattern where a central "Orchestrator" agent manages specialized "Sub-agents" (e.g., for data processing, literature review).
